MY Process

I follow a design-thinking methodology, placing a strong emphasis on user needs
and experiences throughout my process.

  • Brief
  • Research
  • Design
  • Test & Iterate
  • Brief
  • Research
  • Design
  • Test & Iterate

The brief is where I dive into understanding you, your business, and your users. This helps me Design something that works perfectly for you, your business, and your audience.

At this stage, I dive into understanding your audience—who they are, how they interact with your product, and what needs they’re trying to meet. My research methods include market research, surveys, 1:1 user interviews, focus groups, card sorting, and tree testing.

Using the insights gathered about you and your audience, I start shaping ideas. I map out user flows and sitemaps first, then move into wireframing. Throughout the process, I gather feedback to refine and improve as we go

I run multiple rounds of testing and iteration to make sure the prototype aligns with user expectations and the product meets its initial business goals.
